Frequently asked Questions - FAQ

Q: What is this app for?
A: This App is made for gamers or other partys that are participating in the area of E-sports, which can be the Team Members, Coaches, Organizers and more.
Q: Can normal Gamers use this App as well?
A: Yes, you can use this App even if you do not want to participate in Tournaments or want to get into E-sports. But it is required for you in this context to mention this is you Account Information to help other people with their search.
Q: Do you offer any payed Subscriptions?
A: No, I do not offer any Premium Subscriptions yet.
Q: Can I use this App without an Account?
A: No, I strictly forbid users without an registered Account to use any of our Services. This is because the Users on this App are searching for serious reasons and to prevent any trolling or other inappropriate behaviour from non-registred accounts.
Q: Is this Webapp finished?
A: No, I am still working on this Webapp!
